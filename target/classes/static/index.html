Welcome to PlantPal – Cultivating Care and Sustainability

PlantPal is a purpose-driven platform connecting people, communities, and organizations to reduce food waste, promote plant care, and encourage sustainable living. Whether you’re a donor, volunteer, or eco-conscious citizen, PlantPal is here to support your green journey.

Donate surplus food to nearby NGOs or shelters

Find or organize local plant care and recycling events

Track your impact and be part of a greener tomorrow
